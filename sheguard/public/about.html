About SafeHer

SafeHer is an AI-powered safety architecture designed to shield and empower women in real-time. Our overarching mission is to leverage advanced edge computing and intelligent automation to deliver instant personal security.

By blending autonomous stasis verification, local natural language voice processing algorithms, and deep road-network coordinate routing, help is constantly monitored, evaluated, and deployed without introducing network lag or requiring manual inputs.
